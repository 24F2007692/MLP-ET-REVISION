2. The R2 Score Formula

$$ 1 - \frac{\text{SS}_{res} \text{ (Error)}}{\text{SS}_{tot} \text{ (Variance)}} $$
Trap: SStot (Mean se doori) hamesha Neeche aayega.
Negative R2: Model average prediction se bhi ghatiya hai.

3. Macro vs Weighted:

  • Macro: Simple Avg. Listens to Minority.
  • Weighted: Size matters. Listens to Majority.
