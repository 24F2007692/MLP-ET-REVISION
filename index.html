Precision: $ \frac{TP}{TP + FP} $ (Controls False Positives).

Recall: $ \frac{TP}{TP + FN} $ (Controls False Negatives).

Specificity (TNR): Correctly identified Negatives.

Metric Dictionary

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness. Use ONLY if classes are balanced.
  • F1-score: Balance of P & R. Best for imbalanced data.
  • ROC-AUC: Threshold-independent. Ranks Positives > Negatives.
  • PR-AUC: Precision-Recall. Preferred for HIGHLY imbalanced data.
  • Log Loss: Measures probability quality (Confidence matters).

2. The R2 Score Formula

$$ R^2 = 1 - \frac{\sum (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2}{\sum (y_i - \bar{y})^2} $$
Trap: SStot (Mean se doori) hamesha Neeche aayega.
Negative R2: Model average prediction se bhi ghatiya hai.

3. Macro vs Weighted:

  • Macro: Simple Avg. Listens to Minority.
  • Weighted: Size matters. Listens to Majority.
